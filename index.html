Legal DNA testing follows strict chain-of-custody procedures, requires identity verification, and produces results admissible in court or for immigration purposes. Non-legal (peace-of-mind) testing is for personal knowledge only and does not follow those protocols.
Turnaround times vary by test type. Drug test results typically come back within 24–72 hours. DNA results usually take 3–5 business days. Prenatal NIPT results can take 7–10 business days. We'll give you a clear timeline when you schedule.
